Our Project

1.Butterfly Garden

Tagline – Don’t Just Fly
Description lines – Smt. Smriti Zubin Irani, Minister if women and child development on 15th August 2022 inaugurated butterfly garden at manav Rachna University Faridabad. Butterfly garden is made to enhance the population and diversity of butterflies in manav Rachna campus and its surroundings.

2. PAHAL

Tagline - An educated mind can teach many
Description – Manav Rachna Centre for peace and sustainability’s vertical Prakash initiated a project PAHAL by which members of Prakash vertical teach the students of Prakashdeep School (Non – Profitable organisation). This project’s aim is to Education is the key to all the locked doors of the unknown.

3. ANG

Tagline - Don't make trees rare, keep them with care
Description – on 22 April The tree man of India Deepak Ramesh Gaur on the occasion of Earth Day and also inaugurated project ANG by planting tree at Butterfly. A sapling was planted which made the inauguration memorable, Students of the university got the opportunity and took responsibility to adopt, nurture and grow the sapling.

4.Education of Sustainability Development Program

Tagline: An education: A diamond forever
Description - Under Manav Rachna Centre for peace and sustainability an program ( Aadhuniksiksha) started in which Education for Sustainable Development (ESD) empowers learners of all ages with the knowledge, skills, values and attitudes to address the interconnected global challenges we are facing, including climate change, environmental degradation, loss of biodiversity, poverty and inequality.

About Peace & Sustainability Club

UN’s Development Agenda for 2016-2030 i.e. “Transforming our World: the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development” comprising 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and 169 related Targets have been adopted and signed by the Government of India in Sept 2015. There is already a strong interest in, and response to, the ambitious 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development by governments, international agencies, businesses, civil society and other institutions in UN member countries. The SDGs also have strong relevance to universities and Higher Education institutions as education and research are explicitly recognised in a number of the SDGs. University play important role as a convener and facilitator of support processes of SDGs to make impact in our society. Manav Rachna University introduces Peace and sustainability Club as manav rachna university have a strong faith in youth power and their conviction in making a better world. Let’s join hands to attain Sustainable Development Goals and to attain peace by becoming a part of peace club

Everyone are at the most welcome to come and visit our club
