About Us

Manav Rachna Centre for Peace and Sustainability (MRCPS)

Manav Rachna Centre for Peace and Sustainability (MRCPS) a center of excellence of Manav Rachna University which is a visionary step for the Holistic Development of youth. The MRCPS is the epicenter of academic endeavors like: training, research, community awareness programmes in all spheres of peace and sustainability. The centre is the embryo for promotion new approaches towards ensuring sustainable development and peace at local, national and global level.

Butterfly Garden – Don’t Just Fly

Smt. Smriti Zubin Irani, Minister if women and child development on 15th August 2022 inaugurated butterfly garden at manav Rachna University Faridabad. Butterfly garden is made to enhance the population and diversity of butterflies in manav Rachna campus and its surroundings.

05 Sep

PAHAL - An educated mind can teach many

Manav Rachna Centre for peace and sustainability’s vertical Prakash initiated a project PAHAL by which members of Prakash vertical teach the students of Prakashdeep School (Non – Profitable organisation). This project’s aim is to Education is the key to all the locked doors of the unknown.

22 April

ANG - Don't make trees rare, keep them with care

on 22 April The tree man of India Deepak Ramesh Gaur on the occasion of Earth Day and also inaugurated project ANG by planting tree at Butterfly. A sapling was planted which made the inauguration memorable, Students of the university got the opportunity and took responsibility to adopt, nurture and grow the sapling.
